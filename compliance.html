Statement of Compliance

OnlineFriend (hereinafter referred to as "OnlineFriend") operates as a social media application and is classified as a Social Media Intermediary as defined under the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021 (hereinafter referred to as the “Rules”). In compliance with these Rules, OnlineFriend has implemented the following policies and procedures.

OnlineFriend's Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Community Guidelines (collectively referred to as “OnlineFriend Policies”) are accessible within the app under the Profile tab. Users will be informed of any changes to these Policies at least once a year. Additionally, OnlineFriend will notify users annually that non-compliance with the Policies may lead to immediate termination of access, removal of non-compliant information, or both. These notifications will be communicated through push notifications in the Important Notification tab.

To comply with the Rules, OnlineFriend Policies explicitly prohibit users from publishing content that:

  • Invades another person’s bodily privacy or is harmful or harassing, especially towards children.
  • Is patently false or misleading while appearing as fact.
  • Impersonates or deceives others with the intent to cause harm, wrongful loss, harassment, wrongful gain, or fraud.
  • Threatens the unity, integrity, defense, security, or sovereignty of India.
  • Violates any applicable law.

OnlineFriend is committed to deploying technology-based measures, including automated tools and other mechanisms, to proactively identify and remove content that falls within the above categories. A strict No Nudity Policy is enforced, and any content or interactions involving nudity will be promptly removed or terminated as necessary.

Any orders from a court of competent jurisdiction or from an appropriate government agency for takedown or related purposes should be directed to the Resident Grievance Officer via email at anil@onlinefriend.app.

OnlineFriend will remove or disable access to any stored, hosted, or published information within 36 hours of receiving a valid court order or notification from the government or its authorized agencies, stating that such information is unlawful or prohibited by law in relation to the sovereignty and integrity of India, security of the state, etc., as outlined in the Rules.

Upon receipt of a written order from a legally authorized government agency that clearly states the purpose, OnlineFriend will provide any requested information or assistance within its control or possession to the relevant authority within 72 hours.
